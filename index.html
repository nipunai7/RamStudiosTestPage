About Extraction Valley

Extraction Valley Devils’ Curse is a first/third person multiplayer shooter genre indie video game on PC Windows platform which is developed by RAM Studios using Unreal Engine 4.

Chargers will bring down all in their path to get the Trifuel for power and insurgents are highly organized rebels to protect the people from Trifuel energy deployment. What’s your side?

Chargers and Insurgents will compete face-to-face for defeating the devils and grab their Trifuel which is a powersource that has characteristics of three fuels.

Kill your opponent team members to unlock your powerups and fight easily to earn more points and to get more xp unlocking devil masks.
